Rooted in the Sierra Nevada

Bridging the gap between traditional field forestry and modern data science.

Based in Gold Run, California, Steinley Forestry & GIS Consulting is a husband-and-wife team dedicated to sustainable forest management. We believe that the best decisions are made when boots-on-the-ground observation is paired with high-precision geospatial analysis.

With advanced degrees in forestry and years of experience navigating the complex regulatory environment of California, we help landowners, agencies, and conservation groups manage their land effectively.
